How it works

The SoleDrop journey begins by finding a dropoff location near you. If there are none near you, SIGN UP and you'll be notified when one opens in year area!

  • 1. Place your order in the app

    Your place an order in the app and choose the location you'd like to pick up and drop off your shoes.

  • 2. Drop Off

    Drop your shoes off at the nearest SoleDrop. An order tag will be attached to your shoes so we know which are yours.
